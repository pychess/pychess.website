News

New website.

After the previous owners of pychess.org decided to graciously hand over their domain to the PyChess project, we started working on a new and better website. Our previous page (which may or may not be taken down soon), never really recovered from Googles switch from "pages" to "sites". Instead, for the new site, we decided to go with nanoc which has until now proved itself very usable.

After some great coding by Andrew Nagi, and some designing by Thomas Ahle, we can finally release the new website, in the hope it will be useful.
